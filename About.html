The Lookup is a platform that was launched from the leadership competition at the Applied Sciences University by students Mohammad Abu Al-Rub and Jwana Al-Gazu with an idea aimed at choosing the appropriate movie or series for the viewer without spending a lot of time searching on different movies and series platforms. It seeks to facilitate the reservation of cinema tickets in cinemas in amman and to choose the most appropriate ticket price and time , the project was implemented as a website by students Mohammad Abu Al-Rub, Rua'a Masarweh and Lujain Al-Otaibei
